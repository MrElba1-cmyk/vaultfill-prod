## Information Security Roles
- **Information Security Owner:** Accountable for the information security management system (ISMS) and policy compliance.
- **GRC / Compliance Lead:** Maintains security documentation, coordinates audits, and ensures controls are measured and evidenced.
- **System Owners:** Ensure systems are configured securely, monitored, and maintained.
- **All Personnel:** Must follow security policies, report suspected incidents, and complete security awareness training.

## Asset Management
- **Asset Inventory:** Critical assets are identified and maintained in an inventory including data classification and ownership.
- **Data Classification:** Data is classified (e.g., Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted) and handled according to requirements.
- **Acceptable Use:** Assets (devices, accounts, systems) must be used for authorized business purposes.
- **Access Control:** Access is provisioned based on least privilege and reviewed periodically.

## Control Statements (Questionnaire-Friendly)
- The organization maintains documented roles and responsibilities for information security.
- The organization maintains an asset inventory with ownership and classification.
- Access is granted on least privilege and reviewed on a periodic basis.
