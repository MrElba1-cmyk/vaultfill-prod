# Global Privacy Policy (Mock)

**Purpose:** Describe how personal data is collected, processed, retained, and protected.

## Data Retention
- **Retention Principles:** Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to meet legal, contractual, and business requirements.
- **Retention Schedules:** Retention periods are defined by data category and reviewed periodically.
- **Deletion:** Data deletion is performed using approved processes and is logged when applicable.

## GDPR Compliance
- **Lawful Basis:** Processing is performed under a lawful basis (e.g., contract, legitimate interest, consent) as appropriate.
- **Data Subject Rights:** The organization supports access, correction, deletion, portability, and objection requests.
- **Security Measures:** Technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal data (encryption, access control, monitoring).

## Notes for Security Questionnaires
This mock covers standard privacy topics requested during vendor due diligence:
- retention schedules
- GDPR alignment
- data subject rights and security controls
